Privacy Policy for CapGuard

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

CapGuard (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the CapGuard mobile application (“App”) operates and handles your data.

1. What We Collect

CapGuard is a privacy-focused app. We do not collect or transmit personal information. All data stays on your device. The app accesses only the following local data and system services for its core functionality:

  • Usage Access: To show per-app data usage and foreground/background statistics
  • Wi-Fi Network Info: To monitor data usage on specific networks
  • Mobile Data Info: To track data usage across one or more SIMs
  • Hotspot/Tethering Usage: To track how much data is used by connected devices
  • Battery Stats: To show battery usage alongside data trends
  • Permissions Status: To display what system permissions are active

2. What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information
  • We do not use cloud services or third-party analytics
  • We do not show advertisements or track user behavior
  • We do not require user accounts or logins
  • We do not request or use the INTERNET permission

3. Optional Features

The following features are optional and operate entirely on-device:

  • Forecasting: Estimate your future usage based on trends
  • Low Data Mode Automation: Schedule Android’s built-in data saving mode
  • Temporary Data Pauses: Momentarily restrict usage
  • Monthly Reports: View summaries of past usage by app and network
  • Backup & Restore: Locally save and load your CapGuard data
  • Privacy Dashboard: Review permissions and tracked stats
  • Privacy Mode: Blur or hide sensitive app usage when active

4. Your Control

You can:

  • Delete all local data via the app settings
  • Enable or disable features like privacy mode, notifications, and automation
  • Manually export and import backups

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 13. No data is collected or stored that could be used to identify children or users of any age.

6. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Updates will be noted inside the app.
