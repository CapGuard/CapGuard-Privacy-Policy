Privacy Policy for CapGuard

Effective Date: April 2025

CapGuard is a data monitoring and management app developed by StrataLabs. We are committed to ensuring your data remains private, secure, and entirely on your device.

1. Information CapGuard Accesses

CapGuard does not collect or transmit any personal data. All processing is done locally on your device. The app requests access to the following system data solely to provide its core features:

  • Network Usage Stats: To monitor Wi-Fi and mobile data usage across individual networks.
  • App Usage Stats: To display per-app data usage, foreground/background tracking.
  • Battery Stats: To correlate data usage with battery consumption.
  • Hotspot Data: To calculate tethered usage when your device is a hotspot.
  • Device Permissions: To verify required permissions and inform users if missing.
  • Storage Access: To perform local backups and restore of settings and data history.

2. What We Never Do

  • No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
  • No ads, tracking libraries, or analytics are integrated.
  • No user accounts or logins are required.
  • No internet access — the app does not request the INTERNET permission.

3. Optional On-Device Features

  • Forecasting: Estimates if you are likely to exceed your monthly data limit based on current usage.
  • Low Data Mode Automation: Schedules Android’s data saver to activate during certain times.
  • Temporary Data Pause: Lets you momentarily suspend data usage for certain networks.
  • Hotspot Usage: Tracks data used by devices connected to your mobile hotspot.
  • Monthly Reports: Provides app usage summaries, peak days, and trend graphs.
  • Backup & Restore: Allows export and local restore of app settings and usage history.
  • Privacy Dashboard: Shows permission status and feature usage clearly in-app.
  • Privacy Mode: Optionally blurs sensitive data from the screen.

4. Data Retention

All statistics and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sent to external servers. You can clear all local data at any time from the app settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

CapGuard does not knowingly target or collect any information from users under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated to reflect new features or legal requirements, changes will be clearly announced within the app. The app will continue to operate with no internet access.
