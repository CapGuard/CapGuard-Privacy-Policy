Privacy Policy for CapGuard

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CapGuard (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the CapGuard mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

Personally Identifiable Information (PII): CapGuard does not collect or store any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists.

Non-Personal Information:

  • Wi-Fi SSID for identifying specific networks (not stored or shared externally)
  • Data usage per app (read locally through system APIs)
  • User-configured settings and preferences stored only on the device
  • Usage statistics used only for app functionality

2. How We Use Information

All data collected is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Monitoring your device’s data usage across specified Wi-Fi networks
  • Alerting you when usage thresholds are met
  • Displaying usage trends and breakdowns by app and time
  • Customizing the experience through user-set preferences

3. Data Storage and Retention

All information is stored locally on your device. CapGuard does not transmit, back up, or store data externally. Data is retained until the user clears it through the app settings.

4. Permissions

CapGuard requires access to certain system-level APIs to function. These include:

  • Usage Access: To monitor app-level and overall data usage
  • Wi-Fi State: To determine the active Wi-Fi network name (SSID)
  • Notification Access: To send usage alerts

5. Third-Party Services

CapGuard does not use or integrate any third-party analytics, ad services, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third parties.

6. Security

We take data security seriously. All usage data is stored on-device and never transmitted. Users are encouraged to secure their devices with OS-level security tools.

7. Children's Privacy

CapGuard is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years old.

8. Your Control and Choices

  • You can delete all locally stored data at any time through the app
  • You can modify or reset thresholds and network settings
  • You may uninstall the app at any time, which deletes all local data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be reflected within the app and on the official privacy policy page. Your continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated policy.
